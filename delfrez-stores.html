Shopping the Way you like it

Delfrez is a futuristic retail store designed by our experts to offer a unique shopping experience. The elegant ambience is aesthetically pleasing and provides hassle-free purchases to our customers. Delfrez delivers the luxury of selecting from a range of fresh meat (chicken & mutton) at your convenience that is available in different types of portions & in desired quantities.

Delfrez works on a franchise model and caters to customers, who want high-quality, hygienic meat products. Delfrez is currently mobilized in leading metro cities like Chennai, Bangalore, Gurgaon, Mumbai, and Cochin.
