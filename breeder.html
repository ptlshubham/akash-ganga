Breeder


With a grandparent line of the ability, we produce the best-in-class, to obtain in the finest grade broiler chicks to the market, we are into a strategic alliance with a global leader. Our grandparents are endowed with excellent grade and best FCR.

Suguna’s in-depth understanding of your needs and the Indian environmental conditions helps select the right breed of a good lineage. The company’s commitment to provide you with high quality products is further strengthened by its commitment and efforts in the implementation of latest R&D and technology

The parent day old chicks are produced and brought straight to the brooding farm where they are reared in a bio-secure environment. All the chicks are administered with vaccination as per the prescribed guidelines and are nourished with quality feed from Suguna’s own feed mills. Best quality and disease free hatching eggs are further moved to the Suguna’s state-of-the-art hatcheries. Quality is further reinstated by Suguna’s team of experts comprising of Doctorates and Veterinarians. With this you can be assured that the Breeder reaching you is of highest quality

Breeder

At Suguna, we have a healthy breeder lineage to support the production of best-in-class chicks, which are developed into the finest grade broiler chicken with maximum meat yield. The firm enabled/activated a refined R&D and innovative technology management to provide high-quality produce.

The parent day-old chicks are produced and brought straight to the brooding farm, where they are reared in a bio-secured environment. All the chicks are administered as per the prescribed guidelines and nourished with a quality feed. The Best quality hatching eggs are further supplied to the state-of-the-art hatcheries. A team of experts comprising Doctorates and Veterinarians support raising high-quality breeders.

Hatchery

Suguna offers venturing of broiler hatching eggs that are checked with quality parameters for hatchability from the selected flocks. A limited stock of carefully produced and selected hatching eggs is made available to international and domestic customers. The company’s hatching eggs come from a successful parent and grandparent lineage. Quality being paramount, hatching eggs supplied by Suguna is delivered from highly bio-secure farms. With 90% fertility and up to 85% hatchability, Suguna provides only high-quality hatching eggs that match your requirements.

Suguna ensures that the hatching eggs are collected and graded in well-managed farms with scientific poultry management practices and a conservative biosecurity environment. The productive laying quality of the hatching eggs is achieved effortlessly with the company’s scientific poultry management. At Suguna, strict quality checks are held at every stage. Each set of delivered eggs is ensured with the best quality, fertility, hatchability, and egg shell quality. Reach us to know more about Suguna’s commercial broiler hatching eggs venture.
